Mission

To provide first class international Primary and secondary Education for the overall development of the teeming Muslim children at an affordable cost. The Academy assures you of a unique standard and balanced education based on the tarbiyah and tasfiyah model.

Vision

To Impact – with Allah’s support- the knowledge skills and positive attitude necessary for children to grow into life with Islamic virtues and excellence.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum with a focus on science, commercial and arts, catering to students from kindergarten to high school.

Facilities and Resources

The campus features modern classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a well-equipped library. The school also boasts excellent technology infrastructure.

Extracurricular Activities

Students can engage in various sports teams, clubs, and organizations, including quiz and debate.

Faculty and Staff

The school has a dedicated team of qualified teachers and staff, with a favourable teacher-student ratio.

Students Life

The student body is diverse, with opportunities for leadership and student organizations. Support services include counseling and academic support.
