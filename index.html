Mission

To provide first class international Primary and secondary Education for the overall development of the teeming Muslim children at an affordable cost. The Academy assures you of a unique standard and balanced education based on the tarbiyah and tasfiyah model.

To Impact – with Allah’s support- the knowledge skills and positive attitude necessary for children to grow into life with Islamic virtues and excellence.
